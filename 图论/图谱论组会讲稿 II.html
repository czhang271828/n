Spectrum of the weighted graphs

Denote w as a function defining edge weights, i.e.

w:E→R,(u,v)↦w(u,v).

Here w(u,v)=w(v,u)=w(e) is the weight of edge e between u and v.

Thence deg⁡v=∑uw(u,v). In many cases of applications, the graph may equip with self-loops (any examples?). The Laplacian matrix is revised to be

L:=D−A={deg⁡v−w(v,v)u=v,−w(u,v)u∼v,0else.

Hence

L:={1−w(v,v)deg⁡vu=v,−w(u,v)deg⁡v⋅deg⁡uu∼v,0else.
